Welcome to the Playbook

In this section for Brand Managers you'll find all you need to know about your role in digital projects, along with guidance on web development, handy checklists, and advice on how to engage with agencies and Digital Technologists.

This is a playbook, and its aim is to take the hassle out of creating digital project. Take a good look at the sections below and – who knows – launching your project might actually be more fun than you thought.
