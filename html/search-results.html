  1. Diageo Age Gateway

    Age-gates are the first thing most Diageo visitors will see on arriving at a Diageo website. All Diageo sites must have an Age Gate to ensure the user is of legal drinking age in their resident country. Diageo Age Gateway (DAGT) is a centrally hosted best practice solution on Horizon. It is available to any Diageo desktop or mobile website. Agencies can access the Diageo Age Gateway (DAGT) Manual to implement the age gate according Diageo standards.
