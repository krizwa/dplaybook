Our standards and best practices

  • Standards for Brand Managers We don’t have many mandatory standards, but they do cover vital areas – from submitting your business case to meeting Diageo’s global benchmarks for SEO, analytics and legal compliance
  • Guidelines for Brand Managers We want all our digital projects to reach the highest possible standards of design and usability. These guidelines will help you create outstanding websites and campaigns that genuinely build your brands
  • Standards for agencies All you need to know about complying with our global standards, including coding standards, SEO and analytics, accessibility, legal compliance and more
  • Guidelines for agencies We count on our agencies to design and develop world-class web projects. Here you’ll find Diageo’s guidelines on best practices in coding methodology and designing for optimum user experience
  • Standards for Digital Technologists Find all our development standards in one handy place. Get to know our benchmarks on everything from browser compliance to security, web services, responsive design and more
  • Guidelines for Digital Technologists In your role as a DT, it’s vital to understand and have a point of view digital design and user experience. These guidelines will give you valuable insights into best practices in the visual, structural and interactive components of web development
