Ideas

It all starts with an idea - an idea to improve the brand’s consumer journey, which may have come out of a discussion amongst your team, or with the brand team. In some cases, it could have evolved into a formal requirement by the time you are looped into the conversation. Whatever the source, an idea will soon be transformed into a brief.

Read more

3

Planning

These are the steps you need to take at the start of any digital project. Your agency is required to put together a response to the brief that you have received from the Diageo team. Your response must include an articulation of the potential solution, estimated cost and timelines.

Read more

4

Design and production

This phase takes you all the way from early scamps and sketches to fully annotated wireframes, designs and then through to the production of assets. It includes not just designing the look and feel, but designing and producing content and finalising SEO and analytics requirements.

Read more

5

Build, test and launch

This is the phase when the solution that you’ve carefully envisioned and designed will be coded and tested – and brought to life.

Measure
