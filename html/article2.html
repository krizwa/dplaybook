Digital Marketing code

As a producer and distributor of alcohol products, Diageo is committed to marketing responsibly and in full compliance with the rules of our Digital Marketing code.

1What the code covers

The code applies to all activities intended to market our beverage brands. It covers most online marketing formats and platforms, including: apps, blogs, instant messaging, mobile communications, mobile messaging, online advertising, gaming, photo-sharing, relationship marketing, RSS feeds, POS, social networking, opinion sites, and search engine marketing and optimisation. It enshrines our promise to always adhere to our core principles, which means taking full responsibility across the following criteria: age affirmation, content (including user-generated content), UGC moderation, confidentiality and intellectual property, transparency, placement, consumer data, responsible research in social media, accessibility, standard mandatories, information security and digital code compliance.

broken-links.xlsx

2Why it is important

A code of conduct is important to any public or private company trading in the open marketplace. But a business that trades in an environment where the products sold require responsible consumption by its intended customers means it is even more vital that these rules are clear, all-encompassing and binding.
