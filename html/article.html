Horizon Gateway

As soon as you know that you’re going to undertake any kind of customer communication, be it digital or otherwise, the first thing to do is let your DT know what you’re planning. They can help with advice, tips and knowledge that can guide you from launch right through to long-term strategy. (See the link below for DT contact information.)

Your DT will be able to give you useful insight into digital media and how they can integrate seamlessly with above-the-line material. They’ll also help you to frame and scope your digital presence, and give you sound good advice about the best agency to choose for delivering your project.
