Horizon to keep your costs down

We’ve put together a platform called Horizon for you to create and manage your digital projects.

This resource has been inspired by the feedback we received from you and your agencies. We have made it more flexible, easier to use, and more secure than the previous platform. We’re adding new services every month that will help you to improve your digital holdings into the future.

What is Horizon?

It’s the best news for Diageo project management – a portal that will help make your digital projects run a lot more smoothly.

Think of Horizon as Diageo’s ‘one-stop-shop’ for all the tools and resources agencies need to build and deliver digital projects for Diageo brands and markets.

Designed in partnership with Diageo’s agencies, Horizon enables you to create and implement any digital asset quickly and easily. This can be anything from global websites like Diageo.com and Smirnoff.com, to short-term branded competition apps and even internal websites.

It offers a dynamic combination of Amazon cloud technology, flexible support options and all the tools and resources you’ll need to build your digital assets. Syl Saller, Chief Marketing Officer says: ‘Horizon represents a huge step-change for us. It is a simpler and cheaper way of delivering excellence for our digital projects. I want all of our marketers and agencies, without exception, to be using Horizon as the platform for our digital growth drivers.’

2

Tell me why it’s good for me and my brand

You’ll be glad to hear that using Horizon will save you money.

How?

First, costs for cloud hosting and 24x7 support for your digital asset could be centrally funded, which is good news for your budget. You can also look at centrally funded cloud hosting with go-live support, as well as a content management system and other software provided by an agency partner.

Second, Horizon isn’t a product straight off the rack. It features customised tools created specifically for Diageo – like Diageo’s Age Gateway and easy-to-build consumer registration forms. These tools handle Google Analytics through ’Horizon Analytics’, which means you get more information about your brand’s consumers and what they are up to on your digital properties.

This can help you to give them more of what they want, and make sure they spend less time and effort on the things they aren’t interested in.

Third, Horizon offers industry gold-standard Amazon cloud hosting and flexible support options to suit the specific needs of every digital project. Depending on project specification and budget, Marketing and agencies can choose from Horizon Ultra, Alto or Edge options. Nothing but the best.
