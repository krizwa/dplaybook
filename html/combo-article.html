Your role is to enable digital excellence

You are the digital partner for the brand teams. You guide them in all things digital. Key to your role is to lead the agencies through the process outlined in this section, and enable the agencies to meet Diageo standards and work efficiently on the Horizon platform.

Ideas

It all starts with an idea. It can be a brainwave that the brand team may have had for using digital to enhance their brand, or they may have received a proposal from the agency. It could even be an initative that has come out of internal comms from your Digital Technology team. Whatever the source, an idea will soon be transformed into a brief.
Planning

This is when the idea starts to take shape – and to transform itself into a plan of action. The agency should be putting together a response to the brief, which includes a concrete articulation of the potential solution, estimated cost and timelines.

When you review the response from the agency, evaluate the plan, the proposed costs and scope of work, and feel free to challenge any of it. As a member of their working team, ideally you would want to review the proposal with the agency before the brand team reviews it.
Design and production

This phase starts once the brand team has approved the proposal, and ideally after the commercial contracts are in place. This phase takes you all the way from early scamps and sketches, to fully annotated wireframes, designs and then through to the production of assets. It includes not just designing the look and feel, but designing and producing content and finalising SEO and analytics requirements.
Build, test and launch

This is the phase when the solution that the team has carefully envisioned and designed will be coded and tested – and brought to life.
Measure

Now that you’ve launched, stop for a moment and enjoy the feeling of your achievement. But you’re not quite finished yet…
